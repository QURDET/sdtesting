🔐 Google Sign-In

Allow staff and members to sign in with their Google accounts. You need a Google Client ID from Google Cloud Console — create OAuth 2.0 credentials and add your site URL as an authorised JavaScript origin.

ℹ️ How it works

Once configured:

  • Staff can sign into the admin panel with their Google account (set their Google email in the Team tab)
  • Members can sign into the members portal with their Google account (set their Google email in the Members tab)
  • Full Access staff automatically get member portal access when logged in
